Fabio Fiori

Hello there! My name is Fabio Fiori and I'm the founder and creator of Tatooine Times. After attending Star Wars Celebration Orlando on April 2017, I was filled with inspiration and excitement, especially because I was lucky enough to experience the Rebels panel. My creativity was recharged and with the help of my team, Tatooine Times was born.

Star Wars was introduced to me at an early age, and ever since it became a huge part of my life. I watch, read, listen to, play, collect, and breathe Star Wars, both legends and canon.

I grew up playing Jedi Outcast, which is still to this day my favorite game! As far as my favorite Star Wars movie goes...Revenge of The Sith is the one I connect the most with, perhaps because I have watched it so many times growing up; the fall of Anakin Skywalker and the rise of Darth Vader is in my opinion the most emotional and powerful event in the Star Wars Universe.

I am loving the new era of Star Wars. I think it's the strongest it's been and it's because of the incredible fan community across the globe. I am proud to be part of it and am ready to share my Star Wars passion (obsession) with you!

Dan Solo

I’m Dan Solo from the UK. My country has always made a considerable contribution to Star Wars, whether it be actors, locations, props or studios. There must have always been something in the air because Star Wars has been a huge part of my life for as long as I can remember.

As a child I was lucky enough to obtain Kenner Star Wars toys in great numbers. A reasonable portion survived into my adult life and when I discovered the box up in my parents attic, it regenerated a passion that continues with me today.

The Empire Strikes Back is my favourite Star Wars film, but I don’t dislike any of the others. Lucasfilm under the ownership of Disney have so far delivered a mixed bag for me personally. This doesn’t mean I don’t await any Star Wars release with any less anticipation, as I know there will always be moments or scenes that I love.

I aim to bring a lot to the table of Tatooine Times, especially around collecting, and hopefully we can share a blue milk or two as we ponder the latest Star Wars News and theories.
