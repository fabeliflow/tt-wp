Our Mission

Walking the familiar corridors of a Corellian Corvette, you head towards an escape pod to jettison down to the planet's surface. There you will meet the Tatooine Times team where you can learn about Star Wars collectibles and the latest news and reviews from around the galaxy.

Meet the Team

After touching down on Tatooine, it won't be long before you come across a Sandcrawler, crammed full of scavenged Star Wars collectibles. Take a look inside to gain insights into collecting from vintage to modern lines, strategies and reviews.

Scavenge Now

Chalmun's Cantina in Mos Eisley is the place to head to if you need to get off this rock, or simply catch up on news from other space travellers. Head inside to find out what's going on around the Star Wars galaxy.

Gather Intel

Just like the Sarlacc in The Great Pit of Carkoon, we digest just about everything Star Wars. From movies to tv shows, from games to books, all of our opinions can be found in here.
